Welcome

Welcome to our fake birdwatching site. If this were a real site, it would be the place to learn more about birdwatching, whether you are a beginner looking to get into birding or an expert wanting to share ideas, tips and photos with other like minded people.

Getting started

New here? Read our quick-start guide, pick a local park, and grab a simple pair of binoculars. You will be surprised by how many species you can spot in one afternoon.

Birdwatching ethics

Respect wildlife and other people. Keep your distance, do not disturb nests, and leave the habitat as you found it.
